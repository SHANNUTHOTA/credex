## Interview Method

- **Format:** Remote video call via Google Meet
- **Structure:** Semi-structured discovery interviews (open-ended, problem-exploration)
- **Length:** 28–41 minutes each
- **Interviewer:** Shannu Thota (student)
- **Goal:** Understand how engineering leaders and CTOs currently manage AI tool subscriptions and API spend — what pain points exist, what information is missing, and what output format would be most useful from an audit tool

---

## How Transcripts Were Produced

Each transcript was written from interviewer notes taken live during the call, supplemented by key quotes that were repeated back to the interviewee for accuracy confirmation before the call ended. Timestamps are approximate (±30 seconds) and reflect the point in the conversation when each topic was discussed.

---

## Connection to App Testimonials

The testimonial quotes shown on the landing page of the AI Spend Auditor app are direct excerpts from these interview transcripts. Each quote was selected because it summarises the interviewee's single most impactful insight and was confirmed by the interviewee as an accurate representation of their view.
